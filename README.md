#  Hostelhunch

Hostelhunch is a simple hostel kitchen companion made for students who stare at random ingredients in their room and think *"ab isse banaun kya?"*

The idea behind this project was to make cooking in hostels a little easier. Instead of searching through long recipe websites, users can add the ingredients they currently have and instantly get recipe suggestions that actually match what's available.

One thing I wanted to make useful for Indian hostel life was supporting Hindi ingredient names too. So even if someone types *Anda*, *Aloo*, *Dahi*, *Pyaz*, etc., the app understands them and works normally.

---

##  Features

- Add and manage ingredients in your virtual fridge
- Quick-add options for commonly used hostel items
- Filter ingredients by category
- Recipe suggestions based on available ingredients
- Supports Hindi and English ingredient names
- Expiry tracking for food items
- Match percentage for recipes
- Different meal categories:
  - Breakfast
  - Lunch
  - Dinner
  - Snacks
- Clean and responsive UI

## Why we Made This

Hostel students usually don't have many ingredients or cooking options available, and recipe apps often assume a full kitchen setup.

Hostelhunch was made with the idea of:

*"Tell me what I already have, and suggest something I can actually cook."*
